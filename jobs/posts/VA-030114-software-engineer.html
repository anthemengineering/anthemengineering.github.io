WORK WITH US

Software Engineer (Maryland)

APPLY TO THIS POSITION

Have you been working hard to improve your skills with a programming language like Java, JavaScript, or Ruby? Do you enjoy being part of teams with diverse backgrounds that work closely with users to understand their problems and create solutions? Do you seek out projects that make real, tangible impact?


If you answered yes to any of these questions and have a history of making an impact on projects — large or small — then we want to talk to you! Anthem Engineering is looking for collaborative, self-motivated, and highly skilled software engineers to work directly with our customers on a wide variety of technical projects. Your responsibilities will include working with users to define and characterize their problems, designing and developing solutions with your team, and deploying and maintaining the tools you create.

ADDITIONAL DESIRED TRAITS

Education: bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or related field


Experience: eight or more years experience developing Java-based applications for the Federal Government


Skills:
Programming Languages and Technologies: Java, REST, SOAP, SOA

Relational Databases: MySQL, Postgres, Oracle

Frameworks: Spring, J2EE, Struts

Security Clearance: Active TS/SCI with polygraph

  • 19 days paid time off + 10 holidays
  • Comprehensive health insurance plan
  • Yearly training budget to support a plan that you develop
  • Flexible work schedules
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company paid contribution + profit sharing

Top 5 Reasons to Work at Anthem Engineering
