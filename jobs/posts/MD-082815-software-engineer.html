WORK WITH US

Software Engineer (Maryland)

APPLY TO THIS POSITION

Have you been working hard to improve your skills with a programming language like Java, JavaScript, or Ruby? Are you trying to learn new programming languages like Clojure or Haskell? Have you experimented with tools like Hive, Pig, or Storm? Do you enjoy working in teams with smart people who give you honest feedback and are vested in your growth?


If you answered yes to any of these questions and have a history of making an impact on projects — large or small — then we want to talk to you! Anthem Engineering is looking for software engineers who are collaborative, self-motivated, and highly skilled to join our teams. We work on projects that make our customers' lives easier through technology. We work in small teams to deliver capabilities iteratively and agilely. We're always learning and always trying to make a difference.

Join our team helping build Biosurveillance software that helps the government and military respond to and collaborate on pandemic events. You would help build web services, data analytics, and the Web 2.0 front-end.

ADDITIONAL DESIRED TRAITS

Education: bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or related field


Experience: experience developing Java and JVM-based applications for the Federal Government


Skills:
Languages: Java, JavaScript

Frameworks: JPA/Hibernate, Guava, JAX-RS, Node.js, Angular.js, jQuery, Bootstrap

Databases: Postgres, Accumulo, MongoDB, Solr, Elasticsearch

Collaboration and Organization: Git, GitLab, GitHub, Google Apps, IntelliJ, Eclipse, vim, Emacs, JIRA
