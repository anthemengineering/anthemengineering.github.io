WORK WITH US

Systems Administrator (Maryland)

APPLY TO THIS POSITION

Do you enjoy building large computer systems? Is there a cluster of networked machines at your house? Do you argue with your friends about whether zsh is better than bash? Do you have a favorite configuration management tool, like Puppet, Chef, Ansible, or Salt? Have you experimented with virtualized private server providers like Amazon Web Services, DigitalOcean, Linode, or Rackspace? Do you enjoy solving problems and automating solutions for them?


If you answered yes to any of these questions and have a history of making an impact on projects — large or small — then we want to talk to you! Anthem Engineering is looking for system administrators who are collaborative, self-motivated, and highly skilled to join our teams. Our customers depend on us to design, implement, and maintain large virtualized infrastructures, their underlying hardware and network, and the services built on top of them. We need talented system administrators who are skilled at creating automated, repeatable solutions.

Example Responsibilities

  • Managing a VMWare ESX-based virtual infrastructure as requirements for additional capacity, customers, and access grow
  • Reconfiguring portions of such an environment to use OpenStack and KVM without interruptions in service
  • Installing and maintaining large distributed services like Hadoop, Accumulo, and OpenShift in a RHEL or CentOS environment using Puppet
  • Collaborating with customers to identify, design, and deploy additional cloud-based services, platforms, and frameworks

ADDITIONAL DESIRED TRAITS

Education: bachelor's or master's degree in computer science or related field


Experience: experience building, administering, and managing large virtualized computer systems for the Federal Government


Skills:
Operating System: Red Hat Enterprise Linux, CentOS

Scripting Languages: Bash, Python, Ruby

Configuration Management: Puppet, Chef

Virtualization: OpenStack, VMWare, KVM, AWS EC2

Cloud Computing: Hadoop, Cloudera Distribution for Hadoop, Hortonworks Data Platform

Security Clearance: Active TS/SCI with fullscope polygraph
